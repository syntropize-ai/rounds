# Action Center

Action Center is where operators review work that needs a human decision before it runs.

## What appears here

- pending approval requests;
- remediation plans waiting for approval;
- resolved approvals;
- scoped operations tagged by connector, namespace, or requesting team.

The sidebar badge shows pending remediation plans so operators can notice blocked work without opening the page.

## Review an approval

Open **Actions** from the sidebar.

For each request, review:

- action type;
- target service or resource;
- risk level;
- reason and context;
- parameters;
- expiration;
- requester or related investigation.

Choose **Approve**, **Approve & Execute**, or **Reject** depending on the action type and your permissions.

## Plans tab

Use the Plans tab for remediation plans created by background investigations or agent proposals. A plan can include multiple steps and should be reviewed as a unit before execution.

## Filters

Filter approvals by connector, namespace, or requester team when your environment has multi-team operations enabled.

## Permissions

Only users with approval permission can approve or reject. Other users may see a limited view depending on RBAC.
